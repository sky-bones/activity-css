Oh The Colours of Nature

Oh the red petals

  • Roses of the spring

Under the blue sky

  • Auzure depths of summer

Above the green grass

  • Sprouts of early March

Forever nigh

  • Who actually says that

Give me the orange sunshine

  • I'd call it gold

With the red sky

  • Autumn sunsets

Give me the grey mornings

  • Of the early summer day

To view with brown eyes

Give me all of nature's colors

To imprint on my soul

Oh the colors of nature

Will never ever get old

Bad Poetry About Colour

is a carefully thought out masterpiece of how to combine the least amount of rhyming, and fluidity of text known to mankind.
